# КУСАКА

## Register
brand

## Product
Online ordering and delivery page for КУСАКА — a local sushi rolls and pizza delivery service in Цимлянск, Волгодонск, and surrounding district.

## Users
Locals in the Цимлянск / Волгодонск area who want fresh rolls and pizza delivered at home. Straightforward people who care about the food and the price, not the aesthetics of the ordering page.

## Brand personality
Direct, unpretentious, energetic. The name "Кусака" (biter) carries a slightly cheeky, assertive edge. Not premium, not upscale — just good food, fast.

## Anti-references
- Moody dark "premium restaurant" vibes (what the current version has)
- Glamorous gradient-heavy dark themes common to AI-generated food delivery sites
- Warm-cream / beige "artisan" food aesthetics
- Editorial typographic setups with serifs and ruled separators
